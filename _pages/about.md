**Welcome!**

I am a postdoctoral researcher at [UQAM](https://uqam.ca/){:target="\_blank"}, working with [Sébastien Gambs](https://sebastiengambs.openum.ca/){:target="\_blank"}. My research interests are data privacy, optimization, and machine learning. My current research concerns privacy, interpretability, and fairness in machine learning.

I earned my Ph.D. in Computer Science at [Université Toulouse III](http://www.univ-tlse3.fr/){:target="\_blank"}, under the supervision of [Marie-José Huguet](https://homepages.laas.fr/huguet/drupal/node/11){:target="\_blank"} and [Marc-Olivier Killijian](http://homepages.laas.fr/mkilliji/MOK_Home/Marc-Olivier_Killijian.html){:target="\_blank"}. During my Ph.D. I was affiliated to [LAAS-CNRS](https://www.laas.fr/public/){:target="\_blank"}, a member of both [ROC](https://www.laas.fr/public/en/roc/){:target="\_blank"} and [TSF](https://www.laas.fr/public/en/tsf/){:target="\_blank"} research groups, and have worked on privacy enhancing technologies for ridesharing.

More details can be found on my CV [Here](https://aivodji.github.io/assets/pdf/CV_Ulrich.pdf){:target="\_blank"}
